No clear path to a complete phase out from BMW and KIA

How's the automotive companies preparing for that? Of the top ten brands sold in the UK, half have a date similar or sooner than 2030.

The only one with a worldwide goal is Mercedes-Benz, who aims to achieve a complete phase out by the end of the decade.
